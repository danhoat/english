A beautiful day

I'm Dan, I am a freelancer so almost days of the week are the same, not diffrent much.

My beautiful day is not a special day in week. It depends on mood and how is a beautiful day in me?

I wake up at 5:30 and take a moment to meditate, then I go to bath room brush my teeth. After that I warm up and do excersise about 10 - 15 minutes

Next , I get dress a sports outfit and wear soccer shoes.

5:45 I go to the stadium near my aparment to play football with my friends. Includes warm up, break time, training, it finishes at 7h30.


My breakfast is chicken sticky rice, sometimes I eat bread in a familiar sidewalk bread shop.
I use my phone to check social media and read Online newspaper at this time


I come back my room at 8:30, take a shower then start my work day at 9:00

11:00 I go out and have a lunch. After that, I take a nap about 20 minutes. I continue my work at 13:00

I usually turn of my pc at 4:00 AM then go out to relax with my soon. We go to center market or play something outside together. Sometime just go around town by motorbike.

I will come back my room about 8:00 PM. At this time, I have an english class online or do something in my pc of my work or relax only.
